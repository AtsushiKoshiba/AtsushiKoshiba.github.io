Profile

I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Electronic and Information Engineering at Tokyo University of Agriculture and Technology. My research interests are operating systems, resource management for low-power architectures, heterogeneous computing, and parallel computing environment. I am a student member of IPSJ and IEEE.

Academic Backgrounds
  • Mar. 2016, Master degree of Computer and Information Sciences, Tokyo University of Agriculture and Technology.
  • Mar. 2014, Bachelor degree of Computer and Information Sciences, Tokyo University of Agriculture and Technology.
