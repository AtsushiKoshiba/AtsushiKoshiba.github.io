Profile

I am a Research Group Leader in the Chair of Computer Systems at TU Munich (TUM). I received my Ph.D. from the department of Electronic and Information Engineering, Tokyo University of Agriculture and Technology in 2019. My research interests are operating systems, computer architectures, heterogeneous computing, cloud computing, secure/trusted systems, and distributed/disaggregated systems. I am a member of ACM and IPSJ.
